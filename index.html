Fuyan Ma

PhD Candidate, Hunan University

I'm a final-year PhD student in the College of Electrical and Information Engineering of Hunan University (HNU), supervised by Prof. Shutao Li and Prof. Bin Sun. Before pursing the PhD degree in HNU, I obtained my bachelor's degree from South Central University for Nationalities in 2018. My research interests include affective computing, human-robot interaction and multimodal fusion. I serve as a peer reviewer for several international journals, such as IEEE-TAFFC, The Visual Computer and Scientific Reports.
